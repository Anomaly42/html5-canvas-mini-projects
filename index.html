Number Visualizer

A geometric visualization of natural numbers via regular polygons denoting prime factors.
Use left and right arrow keys to increase or decrease the number; use up an down keys to change number by + or - 50

Sorting Visualizer

Visualizes Quick Sort; but the code can be changed to run heap sort, selection sort etc. Interactability in progress.

Brick Breaker

A simple game of brick breaker.

Elastic Band

A visualization of the length of a band stretched across two circles, with points on the circles moving at different speeds. reloading gives a new random pair of circles and velocities.
